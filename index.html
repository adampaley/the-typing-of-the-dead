How to Play

It's the night of the living dead! And the only way to beat them is by having brains too big to eat.


Fight back against the zombies by typing in whatever word appears with the zombie and rack up kills to beat the game.


Each kills rewards you with some time back!


Take too long and you'll run out of time.

Zombies are immune to Tablet or Mobile devices.
